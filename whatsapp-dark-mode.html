WhatsApp Dark Mode

WhatsApp Enhancement

Tools

Role

UI Designer

Project Overview

As an avid user of the popular messaging app, WhatsApp, I (along with many others on Instagram) have always wanted a dark mode for this social media platform.

Timeline

July 2019

Design Rationale

At the time, the WhatsApp team was trying to release a dark mode for the app, but I wanted to make my own version anyway. I decided to use more matte colours as it is easier on the eyes, and having the alternating grey and navy makes message threads easier to distinguish.

Reflection

While this design works for the most part, there can also be improvements. For example, the highlighted blue links on the grey background is a little hard to distinguish. I can look into acceptable colour contrast ratios and work on improving my UI designs to be more accessible in the future as well.
