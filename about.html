Welcome to my website! For the most part, this site is dedicated to showcasing my design work. However...this page is different. Here is where you get to learn about what I do outside of school and design!

I play all kinds of sports.

From a young age, I’ve dabbled in all kinds of sports. Way back in the day, I was a competitive figure skater. Then, I did about 6 years of competitive-level swimming. I’ve also played volleyball, badminton, basketball and participated in track & field as a part of school teams too.

Now, I’m an avid snowboarder and rock climber (honestly, I love anything to do with heights and adrenaline). Here you can see me trying to snowboard with ski poles due to flat terrain (it was an interesting experience...).

However, one sport I can’t seem to grasp is soccer...something about feet-eye coordination somehow doesn’t work for me.

I love (all kinds of) art.

Whether this includes drawing, playing music or creating something new, I love any and all forms of art.

Right now, I’ve been immersed in the craft of quarantine baking (as you can see). I love experimenting with food and I usually help cook dinner most nights as well. Not much more to be said, food is just amazing!
I also love using art as a tool for reflection and planning. For example, I often like to make creative bullet journal spreads to track my day-to-day activities and add inspirational quotes to stay positive.

Last, but not least, I love travelling!

I absolutely love adventure and travelling. I’ve visited places all over the world and my family is big on road trips (fun fact: we have driven through half of the United States). Whether it is cities or nature, I would visit any new place. One day, I hope to visit Amsterdam and eat authentic Dutch food while walking along the edges of the canals!
